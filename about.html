About Us

Jerry is my dad. At eighty years old, his eyesight has declined, and he has also begun to show signs of mild cognitive impairment. Traditional calendar apps became overwhelming. The text was too small, and anything beyond a day or two quickly became confusing.

I created a simple calendar that shows only today and tomorrow, synced automatically with your existing calendar.

The app is read-only, so events cannot be accidentally changed. The text is large and bold for easy reading, and the colors are chosen to be gentle on aging eyes, reducing strain and visual fatigue.
